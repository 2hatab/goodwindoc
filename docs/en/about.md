# About Goodwin Digital Assistant

**Goodwin** is your digital AI assistant for your home chat. It works on **Telegram** and **Max** (coming soon). It makes the neighbors’ chat a place of trust, mutual help, and practical value.

Official website: [goodwinapp.ru](https://goodwinapp.ru/)  
Telegram bot: [@g00dwin_bot](https://t.me/g00dwin_bot)  
News channel: [t.me/goodwinplatform](https://t.me/goodwinplatform)

---

## What Goodwin does

- **Chat member verification** — only real residents in the building group for trust and safety
- **Find neighbors** — by apartment number, car, parking spot, or storage unit
- **Requests to management company** — submit a request and track its status in Telegram or Max
- **Verified service providers** — recommendations for handymen and services from neighbors
- **Orders and services** — place orders for services through the platform
- **Community by interests** — find neighbors who share your hobbies and interests
- **Discussion digests** — short daily or weekly summaries so you don’t have to read the whole thread
- **Answers in private** — answers to common questions and finding the information you need from the chat
